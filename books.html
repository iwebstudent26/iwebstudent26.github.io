Top Ten Books According To Me

(SPOILERS) DON'T READ THIS IF YOU DON'T LIKE SPOILERS

The Hunger Games

Suzanne Collins

The Hunger Games! One of my favorites as you can see, it is in my top 10. In my opinion, the plot is REALLY good. It's about 12 districts, in which each of them chooses two people to represent their district in the annual Hunger Games. The Hunger Games challenges these representatives in a game of survival, with challenges like fire, tsunamis, and unheard of monsters, including oppenents(other districts)themselves. What's more exciting than that? Suzanne Collins gives us great character development, that lets us connect to our characters and understand how they feel in certain situations. Trust me, if your friends tell you to read this, READ IT!!

Warcross

Marie Lu

OMG MARIE LU!!! I got to say, she is my favorite author of all time. Warcross is one of my favorites, as it has a great story plot, and a nice touch of romance. If you're a person who likes intense romance, hackers, or anything to do with computers, this book is definitely for you! Emika Chen is just an average citizen in this world. HOwever, she has a great deal of money on her that she must pay for her loan on the house she is living in. The only way of relaxation is through a game called Warcross. Warcross is a fighting game, made by Hideo Tanaka. When Emika is called to be the computer person for him when someone hacks into the system, Emika must find out the hacker. If you listened to my big deal on spoilers before, you would already know this because I expected you to read this book already. Scroll down for more Marie Lu!
